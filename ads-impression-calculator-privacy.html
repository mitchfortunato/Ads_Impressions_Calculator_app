Privacy Policy

Ads Impression Calculator

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Simple Summary: This app doesn't collect any data from you. Everything stays on your device. We don't track you, we don't store your information on our servers, and we don't share anything with third parties.

About This App

Ads Impression Calculator is a professional tool designed for marketing professionals, media buyers, and advertising agencies to calculate advertising impressions and reallocate budgets across multiple platforms.

Information We Don't Collect

Ads Impression Calculator does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Calculation data or results
  • Any other personal or non-personal information

How the App Works

All calculations are performed locally on your Mac. When you enter CPM values, budgets, or other data:

  • The data is processed entirely on your device
  • No information is sent to external servers
  • No internet connection is required or used
  • All computation happens in your computer's memory

Local Data Storage

The app stores calculation history locally on your device using browser localStorage. This means:

  • History is saved only on your Mac
  • Data never leaves your device
  • You can clear history at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data
  • This data is not accessible to Fortunato Digital LLC or any third party

Network Access

Ads Impression Calculator operates completely offline and does not access the internet. The app is sandboxed and has no network permissions.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for professional business use and is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted:

  • Your data's security depends on your Mac's security settings
  • We recommend keeping macOS up to date
  • We recommend using strong device passwords/authentication
  • No data breach is possible since we don't store data on servers

Your Rights and Control

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal data to:

  • Access or request
  • Modify or update
  • Delete or remove
  • Export or port

All application data resides solely on your device and can be managed through the app's interface or removed by uninstalling the application.

Updates to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • macOS sandboxing requirements
  • GDPR (no personal data collected)
  • CCPA (no personal data collected)
  • COPPA (not directed at children)
